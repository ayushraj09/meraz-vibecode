## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

### About MERAZ 6.0

**Q: What is MERAZ 6.0?**  
A: MERAZ 6.0 is the annual techno-cultural festival of IIT Bhilai. It's a 3-day celebration featuring technical competitions, cultural performances, sports tournaments, and star-studded pronites (evening concerts).

**Q: What is the theme of MERAZ 6.0?**  
A: The theme is "Steampunk: Gears of Glory" - a fusion of Victorian-era elegance with futuristic innovation.

**Q: Where is MERAZ 6.0 held?**  
A: At Indian Institute of Technology Bhilai, GEC Campus, Sejbahar, Raipur, Chhattisgarh - 492015

**Q: When is MERAZ 6.0?**  
A: It's a 3-day festival (check website for exact dates in 2026)

---

### Registration & Passes

**Q: How do I register for MERAZ 6.0?**  
A: Visit the official website www.meraz.in and complete online registration, or register on-spot at the venue.

**Q: What types of passes are available?**  
A: 
- Full Festival Pass (₹400) - All 3 days + all pronites
- Day Pass (₹200) - Single day access
- Pronite-Only Pass (₹150) - Just for evening concerts
- VIP Pass (₹1,500) - Premium experience with extra benefits
- Accommodation Package (₹300/night) - Additional for hostel stay

**Q: What does the Full Festival Pass include?**  
A: Entry to all events for 3 days, all 3 pronites, festival merchandise (T-shirt, wristband, ID), food coupons worth ₹200, and unlimited participation in informal events.

**Q: Can I register on the spot?**  
A: Yes, on-spot registration is available at ₹500 per person (subject to availability).

**Q: Is there a refund policy?**  
A: No, registration fees are non-refundable once confirmed.

**Q: Are group discounts available?**  
A: Yes! 5-10 people get 5% off, 11-20 people get 10% off, and 21+ people get 15% off.

**Q: Can I buy just a pronite pass without attending other events?**  
A: Yes, Pronite-Only passes are available for ₹150 per night.

---

### Events

**Q: Can I participate in multiple events?**  
A: Yes, you can participate in unlimited events if timings don't clash.

**Q: What are technical events?**  
A: Technical events include RoboSoccer, Line Following Bot, FPGA Design, CTF (Capture The Flag), AlgoClash, DSAI Hackathon, BIB Hackathon, Mystery Box, OpenQuest, The Forge, VR+ Drive X, and Sci-Tech Showcase.

**Q: What are informal events?**  
A: Informal events are casual, fun activities held at MSH Road and near Mini Stage throughout all 3 days. They include Radio Meraz broadcasts, game stalls, treasure hunts, carnival games, photo booths, and quick competitions.

**Q: When is the Opening Ceremony?**  
A: Day 1, 9:00-10:00 AM at LH 500

**Q: When and where is the hackathon?**  
A: Two hackathons - DSAI Hackathon (L101, Day 3) and BIB Hackathon (L102, Day 3), both run throughout the day.

**Q: When is the fashion show (Attire Spectra)?**  
A: Day 3, 6:00-7:30 PM at Main Stage

**Q: When is Battle of Bands?**  
A: Day 2, 6:00-8:00 PM at Main Stage

**Q: What sports events are there?**  
A: Futsal (MSH Ground), Volleyball (Volleyball Court), Basketball (Basketball Court), and Box Cricket (Helipad 3) - all on Day 2 & 3.

**Q: When is MUN (Model United Nations)?**  
A: Day 2, starting from 9:00 AM at L105

**Q: Is there a photography competition?**  
A: Yes, Shutter Quest on Day 2 at L104

**Q: Are there team size restrictions?**  
A: Yes, each event has specific team size requirements. Check individual event pages on the website.

**Q: How are winners decided?**  
A: Winners are selected by expert judges, and their decisions are final and binding.
